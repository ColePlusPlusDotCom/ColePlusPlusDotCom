# About Me...


Hello, my name is Cole. I'm 25, and I've realized something about myself.

I cannot continue with my current career for the rest of my life. I always believed it didn’t matter how I made money to support myself, as long as I found fulfillment in my life outside of work. It turns out that isn’t true.

I graduated with a Master’s in Accounting and have been working as a Senior Financial Analyst for almost five years. I am well-compensated and my future is secure. Since graduating, I’ve had more time to focus on hobbies like rock climbing, learning Mandarin Chinese, and spending time with friends. These are the things I thought would give me purpose, but—as difficult as it is to admit—I am still unfulfilled. I’ve realized that my best work is driven by passion, and at the moment, I have no passion to inject into my current field. I suppose this is a consequence of my frontal lobe finally fully developing.

After much deliberation, I’ve decided what I want to be when I grow up. It took 25 years, but everyone is on their own clock. **I am a game designer**. Throughout my life, games have provided experiences that changed who I am; some have even become part of my identity. They offered worlds to immerse myself in where I could forget everything else, and within them, I’ve made friendships that will last a lifetime. To me, this is the magic of video games. It is why I’m willing to risk a stable career for one that is unknown. I only have one life, and the idea of devoting it to sharing that same magic with others fills me with vigor. I want to create.

My greatest skill is my ability to use available resources and critical thinking to solve problems outside of my immediate expertise. That is the how I will bridge the gap between my current career and my future one. This GitHub profile will act as my portfolio. I will post blog-style updates on my game design journey, publish my projects, and discuss the literature I am using to build my knowledge base.

- I love collaborating with others and sharing knowledge; I'm a great listener
